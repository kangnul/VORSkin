About VOrskin
Your Solution For Body, Skin, and Beauty Care

History

Created in 2022, Vorskin is an Indonesian-based e-commerce specifically marketing for beauty products. Our wide range of beauty products include, but not limited to: make up, skin care, fragrance and beauty tools for both man and woman across Indonesia.

Vision

• To promote the highest quality skin care product many companies has to offer
• To provide the highest quality product available for Indonesian people
• To promote vegan-safe, cruelty-free, and non-toxic beauty product

Mission

• To accelerate the growth & development of beauty and skin care industry in Indonesia
• To build an online platform for trusted beauty and skin care product in Indonesia
• To make beauty and skin care product more accessible for every Indonesian
